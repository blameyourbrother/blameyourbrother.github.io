Blame Your Brother Creative Workshop

Our Story

Blame Your Brother is a boutique animation and design shop. Based in Toronto, Ontario, we specialize in 2D classical and 2D/3D digital animation along with developing intellectual property for commercial and broadcast clients.

Our goal is to create compelling and entertaining work by combining the power of design with a story-driven message. Since we're an artist-owned shop, we also bring a personal touch to every project that walks through our door.

Our Team

First thing's first. Yes, we're all friends. But friends that are in complete collaboration with each other. And though we have different skill sets — whether it's art direction, production or animation — we all believe in the power of design and story-driven content. So we all push each other to do great work that's done with great care.

Though based in Toronto, our years working in the animation and advertising communities have allowed us to develop contacts that have a global reach.
