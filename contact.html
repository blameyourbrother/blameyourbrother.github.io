Let's Work Together

Blame Your Brother is a boutique design and story - driven animation studio. We focus on 2D classical and 2D/3D digital animation, and illustrative design for commercial, short-format broadcast projects. We're a dedicated group of friends who like to push boundaries, tell stories, and entertain with great care.

Or swing on by and join us for a coffee. We look forward to meeting you.
