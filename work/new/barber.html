Blame Your Brother Creative Workshop

Animated Short

In the spring of 2009, we had the honour of being selected to direct a short for the Teletoon animated TV pilot "Dunce Bucket". The ask was to choose a brief comedic VO — we selected from a list of 10 — and create a story and visual treatment based on what we heard.
