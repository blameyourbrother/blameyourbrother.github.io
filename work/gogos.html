Gogo's Crazy Bones

Animated TV Spot

  • Share via email
  • Share via Twitter
  • Share via Facebook
  • Share via Pinterest

We had a ton of fun co-producing an international toy commercial for "Gogo's Crazy Bones" with our friends at Data Armada. BYB directed and created the animation while Data Armada shot and directed the live action portions of the spot. Having only less than one month to deliver a 30 and 15 second spot, we quickly put together our talented crew and went to work.

This post is a BYB directors cut only, featuring the animation, and not the final version of the aired commercial.
