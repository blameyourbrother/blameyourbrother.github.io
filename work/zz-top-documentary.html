Blame Your Brother Creative Workshop

So stoked to be asked by Banger Films to direct the animated sequences of their new rock documentary for the legendary ZZ Top! There ended up being 2 minutes of hand drawn animation to tell the story of ZZ Top's producer, Bill Ham. The documentary was full of insight and beautiful cinematography.
